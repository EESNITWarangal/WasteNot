About WasteNot

How WasteNot Works

  1. List Excess Food: Donors (restaurants, individuals, event organizers) list surplus food on the platform, providing details like quantity, dishes, expiry, and pickup location.
  2. Find & Claim Food: Volunteers and organizations browse available food in their city and claim what they can distribute.
  3. Pickup & Distribute: Claimed food is picked up by the claimer, who contacts the donor directly and delivers it to those in need.
  4. Track Impact: Both donors and claimers can view their food donation and claim history, helping everyone see their positive impact.
