How WasteNot Works

  1. List Excess Food: Donors (restaurants, individuals, event organizers) list surplus food on the platform, providing detailed information about food and its quality.
  2. Find & Claim Food: Volunteers and organizations can browse available food in their city and claim what they can distribute.
  3. Acting as a Bridge: As soon as the food is claimed the claiming authority can find the contact details of the donor from history page.
  4. Pickup & Distribution: Claimed food is picked up by the claimer, who contacts the donor directly and delivers it to those in need.
  5. Track Impact: Both donors and claimers can view their food donation and claim history.
