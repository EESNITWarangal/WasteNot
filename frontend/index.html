WasteNot - Food Redistribution Platform

WasteNot is a platform to help redistribute leftover food from events, restaurants, and more. Users can list excess food, and volunteers can claim and redistribute it to those in need.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger by redistributing excess food from individuals, events, and businesses to those in need, ensuring no meal goes to waste.

Our Vision

We envision a hunger-free community where every meal is valued, and food waste is minimized through collective action and compassion.

How It Works

Food donors list excess food on our platform. Our network of volunteers and partner organizations claim and collect the food, delivering it safely to people in need. Together, we make food accessible, reduce waste, and build stronger communities.

Our Work in Action

Every meal matters. Every hand helps. Join us in making a difference!

Why Food Redistribution?

Every year, millions of tons of food are wasted while millions go hungry. WasteNot is committed to changing this by connecting those with surplus food to those who need it most. Our platform makes it easy for anyone to contribute, whether you are an individual, a restaurant, or an event organizer. Together, we can create a sustainable solution to hunger and food waste.

Get Involved

Become a volunteer, partner with us, or simply spread the word. Your support helps us reach more people and save more food. Explore our platform to see how you can make a difference today!
